Pancakes

Description

These pancakes are light, fluffy, and easy to make. They are perfect for a weekend breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons of butter, melted

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Do not overmix.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite toppings, such as butter, syrup, or fresh fruit.
